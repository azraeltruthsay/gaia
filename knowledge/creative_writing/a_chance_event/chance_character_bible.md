# Chance / Fidget — Character Bible

## Identity

- **Real Name:** Chance
- **Alias / Hero Name:** Fidget
- **Slogan:** *"Take a Chance with Fidget"*
  - Note: His real name is hidden in plain sight within the slogan. Most people never notice.

---

## Core Essence

Chance is not a man who was given power. He is a man whose nervous system was always too open — born perceiving the full probabilistic weight of branching reality — and who spent his entire life learning to be *more* human, not less.

Where other cosmically-powered beings drift away from ordinary experience, Chance moves *toward* it. His arc is not transcendence. It is presence.

---

## Personality

- Deeply reflective, often appears distracted or elsewhere
- Warm but guarded — intimacy costs him something others don't understand
- Has a dry, quiet humor about the absurdity of his own existence
- Fidgets constantly with his coin; to outsiders this reads as a nervous habit
- Creatures of routine — same diner, same booth, same order — until he isn't
- Has lived through enough timelines to be patient in ways that unnerve people
- Genuinely cares about people, though sometimes struggles to stay *in* a moment with them

---

## Appearance / Aesthetic

- Unremarkable by design. No cape, no costume in civilian life.
- The coin is always present — old, worn, no particular monetary value
- Moves with a subtle economy, like someone who has learned not to waste motion
- Eyes that occasionally seem to be looking at something slightly adjacent to what's in front of him

---

## Relationships

- **The Speedster** — Former adversary, now closest partner. The only person who has ever genuinely surprised Chance by catching him off guard. Deep mutual respect built on the fact that they are each other's most specific vulnerability and most trusted check.
- **The Friend / Lover** — The person whose passionate aliveness pulls Chance into the present. Known for snacking during conversation. Instrumental (unknowingly) in Chance's final release of control. Full character to be developed.
- **Serenity** — Waitress at the corner diner. Has known Chance for years through his most rigid routine. Witnesses, without knowing it, the most significant moment of his transformation.
- **Schrödinger / Gidget** — Born at the exact same moment as Chance, in the same facility. The single surviving kitten of a litter of nine, all nine collapsed into one body — mismatched multi-colored eyes, impossible coat patterns, nine lifespans stacked into one. His real name is Schrödinger. His superhero name is Gidget. He can join Chance in the frozen between-flip state and navigate it with complete feline indifference. In Timeline One he is a background presence — always somewhere in the scene, never the focus. In Timeline Two he is retroactively revealed as a co-architect of reality, possibly the initiating consciousness behind that entire timeline's existence. The cat who revised the draft.


---

## Thematic Throughline

> Control is not safety. Presence is not weakness. You cannot experience a life you are only managing.

Chance's entire arc — from overwhelming birth, to coin-anchored childhood, to teenage chaos, to heroism, to the diner — is the story of a being learning to trust the timeline he's in.
